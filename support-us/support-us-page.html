Support Us

In The People’s Fund, we believe that the broadest shoulders should carry the greatest burden, and some should not have to carry any at all. Therefore, we use progressive membership fees based on where you live, so a Canadian or a Norwegian for instance pays €15 per year while a Zambian or a Nepali does not have to pay at all – but they are all full members of our organization.

Our administrative costs are less than 10%, so more than 90% of your funds will be invested in microfinance and donated to health and development projects in low- and middle-income countries.

You can choose to contribute monthly or annually.
Every time you make a transaction, a small amount goes to PayPal (1.4% + €0.35 per transaction). Hence, if you pay annually, a higher share goes to support our work.

Annual payments

- less money spent on transaction fees, so more money can go to our work

Monthly payments

Note: If your country is in our low-income category you will have the option of free membership by clicking any of the above options.
